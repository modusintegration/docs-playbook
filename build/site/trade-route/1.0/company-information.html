Company Information

Enter Company Information

Add a New Partner

  1. On the Company Information Page, if you are adding a new partner, enter the name of the partner in the Company Name box.

  2. Click the Save bar.

Enter Contact Information

  1. The name of the partner you selected on the Trading Partners Page (or added on this page, as a new partner) appears in the Company Name box. If appropriate, you can change it.

  2. In the Contacts section of the Company information page, enter the following:

    • Contact type
      (In the dropdown listbox, select Technical, Business, or Both.)

  3. After you enter a contact type, you can enter the following contact-specific information. All fields are portx/glossary.adoc#sects.

    • First Name

    • Last Name

    • Title

    • E-mail

    • Office Phone

    • Mobile Phone

    • Fax

    • Country

    • Address Line 1

    • Address Line 2

    • City

    • State

    • Postal Code

  4. Click Save to save the values you entered in the previous steps.
